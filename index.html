popularity of names emma & emily over time

This data, taken from the state department website, shows the changes in popularity of two female baby names from 2005 to 2017. In 2005, "Emily" was the most popular female baby name, with 3268 "Emilys" born. In that same year, "Emma" was the eighth most popular female name. By 2017, however, the two names almost switched places, with "Emma" at the #1 spot with 2734 babies. "Emily" moved down to #6. data for this graph found here
